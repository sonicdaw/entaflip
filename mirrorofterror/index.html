"It seems you've wandered into the Mirror Of Terror"

Face each other and cooperate. By embodying the person in the mirror world for 70 seconds, you can break free from the mirror's spell and escape.

Decide who will be the one to lead the poses and who follow the poses.

1. For the pose leader, "The person in front of the mirror"

Strike your poses and move while considering your partner. Or you can become an exercise trainer!

2. For the pose follower, "The person reflected in the mirror"

Keep following the pose (same form) without misalignment, and you'll continue to earn points!

  • High-speed synchronization for high scores
  • Misaligned movements plunge the world into the depths of terror (Minus points)
